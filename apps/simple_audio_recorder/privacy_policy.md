# Privacy Policy for Simple Audio Recorder

**Effective date:** June 20, 2026

Simple Audio Recorder ("the app") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Simple Audio Recorder does not collect, upload, or share personal information.

The app may access your device microphone solely to record audio when you start a recording.

## Audio Recordings

Audio recordings created by the app are stored locally on your device.

Recordings are not transmitted to our servers, uploaded to the cloud, or shared with third parties by the app.

You control your recordings and may rename or delete them within the app.

## Permissions

The app may request:

**Microphone permission**

* Used only to capture audio recordings when you choose to record.

The app does not access the microphone when you are not actively recording.

## Data Storage and Deletion

Recorded audio files are stored on your device.

Uninstalling the app removes app data and locally stored recordings associated with the app.

## Third-Party Services

Simple Audio Recorder does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Simple Audio Recorder does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
